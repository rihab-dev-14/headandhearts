About Us

A youth-led movement dedicated to the harmony of culture and nature.

Our Mission

Hands & Hearts was born from a simple realization: our cultural traditions are deeply intertwined with the land we live on. When we lose our connection to nature, we lose a part of our identity. When we forget our traditions, we lose the wisdom of how to live sustainably.

We empower young people to take the lead in environmental protection and cultural restoration. Through collective action, we are building a future that respects the past while protecting the future.

Our Core Values

Community

We believe in the power of "Toloka"—working together for the common good. Unity is our greatest strength.

Tradition

We honor the wisdom of our ancestors and find modern ways to apply traditional ecological knowledge.

Action

We don't just talk; we act. Every small effort contributes to a larger wave of change.
